Daily 60 helps you turn a personal goal into one focused action you can complete each day.

Choose a goal, pick a category, and Daily 60 builds a simple plan designed around consistent daily progress. Each goal is broken into practical steps, and the app gives you a clear action for today so you know exactly what to do next.

Use Daily 60 for goals across health, fitness, career, finance, learning, relationships, creativity, mindset, or a custom area of your life.

Key features:

- AI-powered goal planning that creates a structured path from your goal
- Daily actions designed for focused 60-minute progress
- Step-by-step plans that keep big goals manageable
- Streak tracking to help you stay consistent
- Progress views for active goals and completed actions
- Adjustable actions when you need something simpler or different
- Reminder and preference settings to support your routine

Daily 60 is built for people who want steady, realistic progress without complicated planning. Instead of asking you to redesign your whole day, it helps you focus on one meaningful action at a time.

Whether you want to run a 5K, learn a new skill, save money, build a creative habit, improve your mindset, or work toward a career goal, Daily 60 gives you a daily plan you can return to.

Start with one goal. Complete one focused action. Build momentum one day at a time.
